The Piccolo Section

Caution. a cousin of the flute, the piccolo, is even more of a danger to your ear drums. Don't mess with the piccolo player or you may find yourself deaf in under two minutes. They tend to be seen as pushovers and just a bunch of girls. They can knock you over with just complaints and make you glad you don't play piccolo. This section is always seen in pairs or large groups.
